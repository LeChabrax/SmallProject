# Kolsquare: Use our synchronization to track your affiliate performance from your Kolsquare account

By

Equipe CSM

Published on

13 August 2025

Synchronising with Kolsquare lets you analyse your influencer marketing campaigns using e-commerce KPIs such as sales volume, revenue, and conversion rate per KOL affiliated with your campaign.

Find all your results at a glance in your Kolsquare reporting:

![](https://affilae.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/ks1.png)

**How does it work?**

If you want to sync your Affilae account with Kolsquare, the first step is to contact your Account Manager or the Support team.

Affilae will ask for your permission to share your data with Kolsquare. Once Affilae has completed the setup, we’ll send you the ID you need to enter in Kolsquare.

Simply go to Settings in Kolsquare, then to Integrations. Click the Affilae button and copy-paste the ID sent by Kolsquare:

![](https://affilae.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/ks2.png)

**✨ You’re now connected!**

What’s next? When you create a campaign, just choose the appropriate program in the Affilae field on Kolsquare and the affiliate data will be activated and displayed for each KOL in your campaign.
